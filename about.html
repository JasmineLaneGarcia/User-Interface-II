A Career Collector

A big thanks to Winnie Song for the title idea!

Some of us struggle with answering the question, "What do I want to do for the rest of my life?"

I have never given a straight answer. In fact, I changed the question.

"What do I want to do right now?"

I've filled numerous hats in various fields since I've been old enough to shave.

You may have seen me at the local cellular shop, waving from afar in a giant cell-phone costume.

Or perhaps marching with my fellow sweaty comrades at bootcamp for the army.

Maybe you got a flat tire and pulled into the wheel repair shop to be greeted by a grungey version of me.


But my fallback has always been a tech-y artist.

I say techy artist because I simply love tech as much as I love art!

I have a 7 year background in graphic design and I'm now working to push a little further into tech & design
with web development.


My other big fascination is centered around dragons, which I now have a pet beardie of my own!

Named Runaga, she's a year old chubby sweetheart.


If you ever look me up, just know I've kept all my references;)
